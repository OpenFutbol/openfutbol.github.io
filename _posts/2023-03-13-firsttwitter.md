# Why Twitter for Open Source ?
Twitter is a powerful social media platform that will help us reach a vast audience and connect with like-minded individuals around the world. This is especially true when it comes to the open source community, where Twitter can be an invaluable tool for sharing information, building relationships, and promoting our community projects.

Twitter is a great place to connect with influencers in the open source community, such as developers, project leaders, and industry experts. By following these individuals and engaging with their content, we will build relationships and gain valuable insights into the latest trends and developments in the community. We will also use Twitter to reach out to potential collaborators or sponsors for community projects!
