Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be good at soccer to join?
Not at all! We welcome players of all skill levels, from complete beginners to competitive players. Our focus is on fun, fitness, and community building.
How much does it cost?
Most games are $5-10 per person to cover field/gym rental costs. Much more affordable than joining a league with no long-term commitments.
What should I bring?
Just bring yourself, water, and appropriate footwear (turf shoes for indoor, cleats for outdoor). We provide the balls and set up the games.
How do I find games?
Join our Meetup group for regular updates, check our interactive map, or follow us on social media. Games are posted regularly with locations and times.
Are the games safe?
Safety is our priority. We emphasize fair play, provide basic first aid, and maintain a respectful community environment. Most games are recreational pace.
