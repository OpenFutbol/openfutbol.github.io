Our Mission

Youth soccer infrastructure exists across Chicago, but it's not always easy to find or compare. OpenFutbol is agnostic - we don't favor any organization. We simply make what exists more transparent and discoverable, especially for families navigating options and for organizations that may lack technology resources.

Why This Matters

Many youth soccer organizations, especially grassroots and community-based programs, lack the technology resources to be easily discoverable online. This creates an uneven playing field where well-resourced clubs dominate search results while equally valuable community programs remain hidden.

OpenFutbol applies FAIR principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) to level this field. We believe the infrastructure exists - it just needs to be visible.
