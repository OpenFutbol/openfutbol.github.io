About OpenFutbol

OpenFutbol is an open-source platform connecting pickup soccer communities across cities worldwide. We believe soccer has the power to bring people together, build communities, and create lasting friendships through the beautiful game.

Starting in Chicago with our amazing partners at NBHD Soccer, our mission is to make pickup soccer accessible, visible, and community-driven in every city.

Learn About Chicago Pickup About NBHD Soccer

Powered by NBHD Soccer

🏙️ Neighborhood Focus

NBHD Soccer organizes pickup games across Chicago's diverse neighborhoods, from Pilsen to Logan Square, building connections at the community level.

👥 Inclusive Community

With dedicated coed and women's games, NBHD Soccer creates welcoming spaces for players of all backgrounds and skill levels to enjoy the beautiful game.

🌱 Grassroots Organizing

As a nonprofit organization, NBHD Soccer demonstrates how community organizing can make pickup soccer accessible and sustainable in urban environments.
